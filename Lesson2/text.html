Lesson 2

Let's take what we learned from Lesson 1 and apply it to some front-end stuff. Here, we're going to make a small search box. The goal is to make it with the following requirements in mind:

  • Type some query in the text field, hit enter.
  • Look up if query is in your available data.
  • If it is, show the first picture associated to it.
  • Otherwise, give an alert saying nothing was found.
Give it a try. Enter AJ or Paul in the text field and press enter. You should get a picture of some cool dudes.
